About

My name is Dan Riches and I am a Quality Assurance professional. I have over 10 years of experience in Application Development and Quality Assurance. My goal is to produce high quality software that functions as intended and performs well.

QA is my primary occupation, but I also do a little freelance web design and front end development on the side. I usually have some sort of project going on whether it is a personal project or a professional one. So on some evenings and weekends I might be working on some sort of web project.
